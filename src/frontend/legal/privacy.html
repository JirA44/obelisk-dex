Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

Privacy by Design

Obelisk DEX is built with privacy as a core principle. As a decentralized, non-custodial platform, we minimize data collection and never have access to your private keys or funds.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect minimal information directly from you:

  • Wallet Address: Your public blockchain address when you connect your wallet (this is public on the blockchain)
  • Preferences: UI settings like theme, language, and display preferences (stored locally)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Data Type Purpose Storage
IP Address Security & rate limiting Not stored
Browser Type Compatibility Not stored
Transaction Data Display history Local storage only
Usage Analytics Improve service Anonymized

1.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Private keys or seed phrases
  • Personal identification documents
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Social security numbers or government IDs
  • Bank account information

2. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the Platform
  • Display your transaction history (from blockchain)
  • Improve user experience and fix bugs
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Blockchain Transparency

Please understand that blockchain transactions are:

  • Public: All transactions are visible on the blockchain
  • Permanent: Transactions cannot be deleted or modified
  • Traceable: Wallet addresses can potentially be linked to identities

We do not control or have access to blockchain data beyond what is publicly available.

4. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save:

  • Theme and language preferences
  • Recently used tokens
  • Slippage settings
  • Connected wallet information (public address only)

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Platform interacts with third-party services:

  • DEX Aggregators: 1inch, 0x Protocol (for swap quotes)
  • Price Oracles: CoinGecko, Chainlink (for price data)
  • RPC Providers: Public RPC endpoints (for blockchain interaction)
  • Wallet Providers: MetaMask, WalletConnect (for wallet connection)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Platform to function
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
  • Regular security audits
  • No server-side storage of sensitive data

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your local data (via browser settings)
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Data portability

Since we collect minimal data and it's stored locally, you have direct control over most of your information.

9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10. International Users

The Platform is accessible globally. By using the Platform, you consent to the processing of any data you provide in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through our official channels.
