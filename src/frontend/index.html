Impermanent Loss (IL)

Definition: Loss incurred when providing liquidity to an AMM pool due to price divergence between assets. Called "impermanent" because it only becomes permanent when you withdraw.

How it works: When you deposit 2 tokens (e.g., ETH/USDC) in a liquidity pool, the AMM automatically rebalances them as prices change. If ETH goes up 100%, the pool sells ETH and buys USDC to maintain the ratio. You end up with less ETH than if you had just held it.

Example: Deposit $1000 (0.5 ETH + 500 USDC). ETH doubles to $2000. Without LP: 0.5 ETH = $1000 + 500 USDC = $1500 total. With LP: Pool rebalances to ~0.35 ETH + 707 USDC = $1414. IL = $86 (5.7%).

Formula: IL = 2 * sqrt(price_ratio) / (1 + price_ratio) - 1
- 25% price change = 0.6% IL
- 100% price change = 5.7% IL
- 400% price change = 20% IL

Mitigation: Choose stable pairs (USDC/DAI), use concentrated liquidity, or pick high-fee pools where fees > IL.

TVL (Total Value Locked)

Total value of assets deposited in a DeFi protocol. Higher TVL = more liquidity and trust.

Yield Farming

Strategy of moving assets between DeFi protocols to maximize returns through liquidity provision, staking, and reward tokens.

Autonomous Venue

Definition: A self-sovereign trading platform that operates with its own internal liquidity, independent of external exchanges. Obelisk functions as an autonomous venue with its own perpetuals engine, AMM, and matching engine.

How it works: Instead of routing all orders to external DEXs (GMX, Hyperliquid, etc.), Obelisk maintains a $100K USDC internal liquidity pool supporting 36 trading pairs. Orders can be executed internally with zero network latency and minimal fees.

Key components:
- Perpetuals Engine: Internal perps with up to 50x leverage, funding rates, and liquidation
- AMM: Constant-product market maker (x*y=k) with 0.3% swap fee
- Matching Engine: P2P order matching between users (Paper/Pool/P2P modes)
- Venue Capital: Dedicated capital allocation per trading bot or user

Advantages: Instant execution, no gas fees on internal trades, full control over liquidity, and independence from external exchange downtime or restrictions.

Venue Capital

Definition: A system for allocating dedicated trading capital to specific bots or strategies within the Obelisk venue.

How it works: Each bot (e.g., MixBot) receives a fixed capital allocation (e.g., $5). The system tracks equity, positions, PnL, and enforces risk limits (max leverage, daily drawdown) independently per venue participant.

Features: Real-time equity tracking, position-specific TP/SL, max 3% daily drawdown protection, and isolated margin per bot. Capital is deposited via /api/trade/venue/deposit and monitored via /api/trade/equity.

Internal Liquidity Pool

A self-managed pool of assets that provides liquidity for trades within the platform, without relying on external exchanges. Obelisk's pool holds $100K USDC across 36 coins, enabling instant order execution. Liquidity providers earn fees from every internal trade.

Internal Matching Engine

System that matches buy and sell orders between users within the platform. Operates in 3 modes: Paper (simulated fills), Pool (filled against internal liquidity), P2P (matched directly between users). Eliminates external exchange fees and latency.

OBL Token

Obelisk's native utility token. Used for governance voting, staking (12-36% APY), and trading fee discounts (10-75% depending on tier: Bronze 100 OBL, Silver 1K, Gold 10K, Platinum 100K).

Micro-Investing

Investing very small amounts (as low as $0.01) with auto-allocation strategies. Obelisk offers 5 strategies: Balanced, Safe, Blue Chip, Degen, and Yield Max, each diversifying across 3-5 tokens automatically.

Overcollateralized Loan

A loan where collateral exceeds the borrowed amount (e.g., 150%). If collateral value drops below the threshold, the position is liquidated to repay the loan. Safer than undercollateralized lending.

Funding Rate

Periodic payment between long and short traders in perpetual contracts. Positive = longs pay shorts (bullish market). Negative = shorts pay longs. Keeps perpetual price anchored to spot price.

AMM (Automated Market Maker)

Smart contract that provides liquidity using a mathematical formula (x * y = k). Replaces traditional order books. Liquidity providers deposit token pairs and earn swap fees (typically 0.3%).

Fiat On-Ramp

Service that converts traditional currency (EUR, USD) into cryptocurrency. Obelisk integrates MoonPay and Transak for direct card-to-crypto purchases without leaving the platform.

Smart Routing

Automatic selection of the best trading venue based on fees, liquidity, and execution speed. Obelisk routes orders across 7+ venues (Lighter, Morpher, Hyperliquid, GMX, MUX, Gains, AsterDEX) to minimize costs.

Protected Vault

Investment product with 90-100% capital protection. Your principal is largely safe while still earning yield. Lower returns than high-risk strategies but ideal for conservative investors.
